EBB SB2209 (CAN) RP2040 cooling

The EBB SB2209 CAN (RP2040) overheats, which can lead to reduced extruder torque, lost extruder steps, and in extreme cases MCU crashes. It needs active cooling, especially in an enclosed machine printing high-temperature plastics like ABS.

This repo contains a description of how I added active (forced air) cooling of the EBB SB2209 CAN (RP2040) in the Stealthburner toolhead of my Voron 2.4.

The problem

Intermittent failure to extrude

Extrusion would sometimes stop briefly and then restart, and sometimes would stop and then never restart.
